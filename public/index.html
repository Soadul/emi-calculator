User Manual & Instructions

Adding a New Customer

Before you can sell a product on EMI, you must add the customer to the database. Navigate to the Add Customer tab, enter their exact phone number and personal details. The phone number acts as their unique ID.

Recording a New Sale

Go to the New Sale tab. Follow these steps:

  • Enter the customer's phone number and click Verify. This ensures the customer exists.
  • Enter the Invoice ID, Product Name, and other product details.
  • Enter the Total Price, Down Payment, and Tenure (in months). The system will automatically calculate the Monthly EMI.
  • Click Confirm & Save Sale to register the EMI contract.

Collecting Payments

When a customer comes to pay their monthly installment:

  • Navigate to the Search & Payments tab.
  • Search for their phone number. You will see a list of all products they have purchased.
  • Find the Active product and click the green Record Payment button.
  • The system will auto-fill the suggested EMI amount, but you can change it if they are paying a different amount. Click Confirm.

Note on Total Paid: The system automatically calculates how much the customer has paid in total by subtracting their Remaining Balance from the initial Total Price.
